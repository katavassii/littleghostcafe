About Us

Once per season we set up a little cafe in an interesting location for a week. We focus on creating pleasant atmospheres in unique places. To help foster creative conversation, all of our tables are covered in paper and we make custom scented crayons for people to draw with as they talk and drink their coffee.

A unique atmosphere is as important as the products themselves. We believe that every detail matters in creating a welcoming, friendly environment that promotes creativity and self-expression. We take pride in our products, and we hope to provide you with an experience you can fondly look back on.
